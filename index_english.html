How does Lottus Wearable work?

  • 1
    One click

    Press the lottus button once in order to send a relief message to your emergency contacts and make them know you are okay

  • 2
    Two click

    Two clicks will create a yellow emergency message and will send a pre defined message by you to your emergency contacts and to our certified lottus users and will let them now your location. This will make them know you may need help in any moment

  • 3
    Three clicks

    Red message mode. Your Lottus button will automatically contact the mergency services, police and all your contacts. They will now your live location and the record mode on your phone will be automatically activated. We will manage the situation so you don't have to worry.

FAQ's

Can I ask for help when my phone is locked?

Of course, by pressing the volume buttons during 5 seconds and having logged in previously in the lottus app.

Do I have to pay something for the App?

No, our Lottus App is free and completly open for everyone. Everyone can use all Lottus Safety app functionalities completly free. Of course the smart button functionalities won't be available unless you've purchased one of our smart accesories.

What are the users LottusHelp?

Lottus Help usere, are uesers who use or have purchased the lottus button, have been verified by ID and can help in an emergencies. As soon as an emergency is registered, LottusHelp users will also receive the help notification. This allows a much faster and more global action. Furthermore, all these users are located at the moment the alarm is activated and both their position and identity are known at all times.

Can I create groups in the Lottus Chat?

Of course, in fact a pre determined chat group is created once you set your 5 emergency contacts.

Can I cancel an alert?

In case everything is a false alarm you will just have to press the lottus button again and everyone will now you are safe and sound, just as we like it!

Can I turn off some of the functionalities?

Yes, all the functionalities included in both the App and our help button are completely optional. All functionalities can be turned off and configured according to your needs through the application. However, all the features we offer have been selectively chosen through numerous sessions asking all kinds of users what your needs are. For this reason, we recommend you, leaving the basic functionalities activated, since we have not chosen them, you did.
